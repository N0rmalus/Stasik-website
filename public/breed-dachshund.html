Dachshund

lightbulb About

The Dachshund, often affectionately referred to as the "wiener dog" or "sausage dog," is a small breed with a big personality. Known for their elongated bodies and short legs, Dachshunds are popular for their charming and playful nature. They come in various coat types, including smooth, longhaired, and wirehaired, and a wide range of colors.

Dachshunds make loving and loyal pets, and their unique appearance and spirited personalities have endeared them to dog lovers around the world.

Life Expectancy: 12 - 16 years
Health: Very healthy doggies

attractions Fun Fact

Dachshunds have a rich history and were originally bred in Germany for hunting badgers. Their name, "Dachshund," translates to "badger dog" in German.

pet_supplies Favourite Foods

Dachshunds, like many dogs, enjoy a balanced diet. Some of their favorite foods include high-quality dog food, lean meats, vegetables, and occasional treats. Always monitor their food intake to maintain a healthy weight.
